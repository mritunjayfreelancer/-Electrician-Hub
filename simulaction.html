⚡ 3-Phase Drag, Wire & Run Simulator

Kaise Khele (Instructions):
1. 📦 **Drag Components:** Kisi bhi block ke center me click karke use screen par kahin bhi drag karein.
2. 🔌 **Wiring:** Component ke corners par bane 🔴 Terminals par click karke dusre terminal tak line khinchein.
3. 🕹️ **Run:** MCB ke andar bane [ON/OFF] Switch par click karein. Agar 3-Phase Motor ke teeno terminals (R, Y, B) sahi se MCB se connect hue, to motor ghumne lagegi!
