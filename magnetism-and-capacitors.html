ITI Electrician Theory Module 05

Magnetism & Capacitors (चुंबकत्व और कैपेसिटर)

👨‍🏫 Classroom Note: Pyare dosto, Magnetism aur Capacitors electrical engineering ki jaan hai. Transformers, Motors aur Generators bina magnetism ke nahi chal sakte, aur Fan ya single-phase motor bina capacitor ke start nahi ho sakti. Is chapter ko aasan aur mazedar bhasha me pure concepts ke sath samjhaya gaya hai. Ekdam dhyan se padhna!

Chapter 1 : Magnetism Aur Uske Niyam

Magnet (चुंबक) Kya Hota Hai?

Magnet ek aisi cheez hai jo Iron, Cobalt, aur Nickel jaise magnetic materials ko apni taraf attract karti hai. Magnet ke do poles hote hain—**North Pole (N)** aur **South Pole (S)**.

🧲 Like Poles (N-N / S-S): Aapas me ek doosre ko door bhagate hain (Repel).
🤝 Unlike Poles (N-S): Aapas me ek doosre ko attract karte hain.
🌍 Freely Suspended Magnet: Agar kisi magnet ko dhage se bandhkar latkaya jaye, to wo hamesha North-South direction me hi rukega.
Magnetic Flux ($\Phi$) aur Field Lines
Magnetic Flux is the total number of magnetic lines of force passing through a specific area. Unit: Weber (Wb).
Dhyan dene wali baat: Magnet ke bahar lines hamesha North se South ki taraf jati hain, aur magnet ke andar ye lines South se North ki taraf travel karti hain. Ye lines kabhi bhi ek doosre ko cross (kat-ti) nahi hain.

Electromagnetism Aur Rules

Electromagnetism Kya Hai?

Jab kisi conductor (wire) se electric current pass hota hai, to us wire ke charo taraf ek temporary magnetic field ban jata hai. Is phenomenon ko **Electromagnetism** kehte hain.

Direction Janne Ke Do Main Rules:
1. Right-Hand Thumb Rule: Agar hum kisi wire ko right hand me pakdein aur thumb current ki direction dikhaye, to ghoomi hui ungliyan (fingers) magnetic field ki direction batayengi.
2. Maxwell's Corkscrew Rule: Agar ek corkscrew ko current ki direction me ghumaya jaye, to screw ke ghoomne ki direction hi magnetic field ki direction hogi.

Magnetic Circuits Ke Important Terms

Jaise electrical circuit me Voltage aur Resistance hote hain, waise hi magnetic circuit me niche diye gaye terms hote hain:

1. MMF (Magnetomotive Force):
MMF is the force that drives magnetic flux through a magnetic circuit. Unit: Ampere-Turns (AT).
MMF = N × I (Turns × Current)
2. Reluctance (S):
Reluctance is the opposition offered to the flow of magnetic flux. Unit: AT/Wb.

Jaise electrical wire me current ko rokne ke liye Resistance hota hai, waise hi magnetic path me flux ko rokne ke liye **Reluctance** hota hai.

Capacitor Aur Uska Principle

Capacitor (कंडेनसर) Kya Hai?
A capacitor is an electronic component that stores electrical energy in the form of an electrostatic field.

Jab do conducting plates ko ek insulating material (jise Dielectric kehte hain, jaise Air, Paper, Mica) ke threw alag kiya jata hai, to wo ek capacitor banta hai. Iski energy store karne ki capacity ko **Capacitance** bolte hain aur iski unit **Farad (F)** hoti hai.

Q = C × V | C = Q / V

Yahan $Q = \text{Charge (Coulomb)}$, $C = \text{Capacitance (Farad)}$, aur $V = \text{Voltage (Volt)}$ hai.

Capacitor Ke Series Aur Parallel Combinations

Dhyan rakhein: Capacitor ka formula Resistor ke formula se bilkul **ulta (opposite)** hota hai. Exams me yahan bache galti karte hain!

Series Combination (श्रेणी संयोजन):

Isme total capacitance drop ho jati hai.
Formula: $$1/C_T = 1/C_1 + 1/C_2 + 1/C_3$$

Parallel Combination (समांतर संयोजन):

Isme total capacitance badh jati hai.
Formula: $$C_T = C_1 + C_2 + C_3$$

Numerical Example: Agar 6$\mu$F aur 12$\mu$F ke do capacitors series me jude hain, to total capacitance kya hogi?
⚡ $1/C_T = 1/6 + 1/12 \implies 2/12 + 1/12 = 3/12 \implies C_T = 12/3 = \mathbf{4\mu\text{F}}$

Chapter Summary (One-Line Revision)

🧲 Flux Unit = Weber (Wb) aur Flux Density ki unit Tesla ($T$) ya $\text{Wb/m}^2$ hoti hai.
🔄 Magnetic Lines Direction = Bahar North se South, Andar South se North.
⚡ MMF Formula = $N \times I$ (Unit: Ampere-Turns).
🚧 Reluctance = Flux ke raste ki rukavat (Unit: AT/Wb).
🔋 Capacitor Stores = Electrostatic field energy store karta hai.
📐 Capacitance Unit = Farad ($F$), aam taur par $\mu\text{F}$ ya $\text{pF}$ use hota hai.
🔌 Series Capacitors = $$1/C_T = 1/C_1 + 1/C_2$$
📈 Parallel Capacitors = $$C_T = C_1 + C_2$$ (Direct sum hota hai).
⚠️ Polarity Caution = Electrolytic capacitors ko hamesha sahi +/- terminal par lagayein, nahi to blast ho sakta hai.

5. Master CBT Top 10 Exam Core Q&A Bank

Q1. Magnetic Flux Density ki SI unit kya hoti hai?
Ans: Tesla ($T$) ya $\text{Wb/m}^2$.
Q2. Permeability ka magnetic circuit me kya kaam hota hai?
Ans: Ye material ki flux conduct karne ki ability hoti hai, jaise electrical circuit me Conductivity hoti hai.
Q3. Kaun sa material permanent magnet banane ke liye sabse best mana jata hai?
Ans: Alnico (Aluminium, Nickel, Cobalt alloy) aur Steel.
Q4. Residual Magnetism (अवशिष्ट चुंबकत्व) kya hota hai?
Ans: Current supply band karne ke baad bhi core me jo thoda sa magnetism bacha reh jata hai, use residual magnetism kehte hain. DC Generators me ye zaroori hai.
Q5. Capacitor ki capacitive reactance ($X_C$) ka DC supply par kya maan hota hai?
Ans: DC supply par frequency zero ($0\text{ Hz}$) hoti hai, isliye $X_C = 1 / (2\pi fC) = \text{Infinite } (\infty)$. Yaani capacitor DC ko completely block kar deta hai.
Q6. Dielectric Strength ki standard unit kya hoti hai?
Ans: $\text{kV/mm}$ (Kilo-volt per millimeter).
Q7. Ceiling Fan me lagne wale starting capacitor ki value lagbhag kitni hoti hai?
Ans: $2.25\,\mu\text{F}$ se $2.5\,\mu\text{F}$ tak.
Q8. Agar do metal plates ke beech ki doori (distance) kam kar di jaye, to capacitance par kya asar padega?
Ans: Capacitance badh jayegi kyunki $C$ distance ($d$) ke inversely proportional ($C \propto 1/d$) hota hai.
Q9. Hysteresis Loss kya hota hai?
Ans: Magnetic material ke baar-baar magnetize aur demagnetize hone se jo heat ke roop me energy loss hoti hai, use Hysteresis Loss bolte hain.
Q10. Single-phase motor me capacitor ka primary functional objective kya hai?
Ans: Phase split karke starting torque paida karna taaki motor apne aap ghoom sake.
