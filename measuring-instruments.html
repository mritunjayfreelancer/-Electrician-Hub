ITI Electrician Theory Module 11

Measuring Instruments (मापक यंत्र)

👨‍🏫 Classroom Note: Dosto, Measuring Instruments ITI Electrician Trade ka sabse important topic hai. Agar tum isko achhe se samajh gaye to wiring, motor, transformer, maintenance aur electrical testing sab easy ho jayega. Ek-ek instrument ko detail me aur aasan tarike se dekhenge!

1. Measuring Instruments Kya Hote Hain?

Measuring instruments are devices used to measure electrical quantities such as voltage, current, resistance, power and insulation resistance.
Aasan Bhasha me: Measuring instruments wo devices hote hain jo electrical circuit ki physical values (Jaise Current, Voltage, Power) ko accurately measure karke scale par show karte hain.

2. Ammeter (एमीटर)

Principle: Ammeter current ke magnetic effect par work karta hai. Jab isme se current flow hota hai to magnetic field produce hoti hai jo pointer ko scale par move karti hai.

Internal Construction & Resistance:

Ammeter ka internal resistance Bahut Low hota hai.
• Ideal Ammeter Resistance = $0\text{ }\Omega$
• Practical Ammeter Resistance = $0.01\text{ }\Omega \text{ to } 0.1\text{ }\Omega$

⚠️ CRITICAL RULE: Ammeter ko hamesha Series (श्रेणी) me lagaya jata hai taaki pura load current iske andar se guzar sake. Agar tumne isko **Parallel** me laga diya, to low resistance hone ke karan bhayanak **Short Circuit** hoga aur meter instantly jal jayega!
[Battery +] ---- [Ammeter] ---- [Load] ---- [Battery -] | (Moving Coil) | Low Shunt Resistor
Ammeter Series Connection Grid

3. Voltmeter (वोल्टमीटर)

Principle: Yeh circuit ke do points ke beech ka Potential Difference (विभवान्तर) measure karta hai. Iske coil ke series me ek bahut high value resistance lagaya jata hai jise **Multiplier** kehte hain.

Internal Resistance Framework:

Voltmeter ka internal resistance Very High hota hai taaki yeh circuit se khud koi current na consume kare.
• Ideal Voltmeter Resistance = $\infty\text{ }\Omega$ (Anant)
• Practical Voltmeter Resistance = $10\text{ k}\Omega \text{ to } 10\text{ M}\Omega$

⚡ Connection Rule: Voltmeter ko hamesha **Parallel (समांतर)** me lagaya jata hai. Agar isko Series me laga diya, to high resistance ki wajah se circuit me current flow block ho jayega aur galat reading aayegi.
[+] -------------------------> [Load] | | [ Voltmeter ] | | | [-] -------------------------> [Ground]
Voltmeter Parallel Network Architecture

4. Wattmeter (वाटमीटर)

Wattmeter is used to measure electrical active power in a circuit.

Coil Architecture: Wattmeter ke andar do main coils hoti hain:

🌀 1. Current Coil (CC): Yeh mote wire aur kam turns ki hoti hai, ise load ke sath Series me joda jata hai.
🌀 2. Pressure Coil / Voltage Coil (PC): Yeh patle wire aur zyada turns ki hoti hai, ise load ke across Parallel me joda jata hai.

In dono coils ke magnetic interaction se pointer move karta hai aur direct True Power ($P = VI \cos\theta$) scalable reading milti hai.

5. Multimeter (मल्टीमीटर) - Multi-Purpose Tool

Yeh ek aisa single instrument hai jo alag-alag modes par bohot saari electrical quantities ko naap sakta hai. Isko AVO (Ampere-Volt-Ohm) meter bhi kehte hain.

Symbols & Interpretation Grid:
• V⎓ : DC Voltage Mode
• V~ : AC Voltage Mode
• A⎓ : DC Current Mode
• Ω : Resistance Mode
• 🔔 (Beep) : Continuity Testing Mode (Wire toota hai ya nahi check karne ke liye)
🚨 Multimeter Safety Warning:

Hamesha range test karte waqt highest range se start karo. Kabhi bhi live voltage line par Resistance (Ω) mode select karke check mat karna, multimeter ka internal fuse ud jayega!

6. Megger (मेगर / इंसुलेशन टेस्टर)

Megger is a portable instrument used to measure very high insulation resistance of cables, motors, and transformers.

Working Mechanism: Normal multimeter low voltage par chalta hai par Megger ke andar ek in-built hand-driven **DC Generator** hota hai jo high voltage ($250\text{V}$, $500\text{V}$, ya $1000\text{V}$) produce karta hai taaki leakage path ka pta chal sake.

📈 Insulation Standards:
• Ek achhe aur safe installation ka insulation resistance hamesha 1 Mega-Ohm ($1\text{ M}\Omega$) se upar hona chahiye.
• Industrial systems me yeh $100\text{ M}\Omega+$ tak target kiya jata hai.

Chapter Summary (One-Line Revision Grid)

• Ammeter = Series me judega, resistance kam hoga, current naapega.
• Voltmeter = Parallel me judega, resistance high hoga, voltage naapega.
• Wattmeter = Current Coil Series + Potential Coil Parallel = Power Measurement.
• Megger = High DC voltage generate karega, Insulation Resistance naapega ($M\Omega$).
• Continuity Mode = Multimeter me beep ki awaaz aane par path closed (healthy) mana jata hai.

5. Master CBT Top 10 Exam Core Q&A Bank

Q1. Ammeter ko series me hi kyu lagaya jata hai?
Ans: Kyunki current ko measure karne ke liye poora circuit current meter se guzarana chahiye. Iska internal resistance bohot kam hota hai.
Q2. Voltmeter ko parallel me na lagakar series me lagane par kya hoga?
Ans: Voltmeter ka internal resistance bohot high hota hai, isliye agar ise series me lagaya to yeh circuit me current flow block kar dega aur reading zero/galat aayegi.
Q3. Ideal Ammeter aur Ideal Voltmeter ka resistance kitna hota hai?
Ans: Ideal Ammeter का Zero ($0\text{ }\Omega$) aur Ideal Voltmeter ka Infinite ($\infty\text{ }\Omega$) hota hai.
Q4. Wattmeter ke andar kaun si do main coils bani hoti hain?
Ans: Current Coil (CC) jo series me lagti hai, aur Pressure Coil (PC) jo parallel me connect hoti hai.
Q5. Megger normal multimeter se alag kaise kaam karta hai?
Ans: Multimeter low-voltage par kaam karta hai, jabki Megger high DC voltage ($250\text{V}$ to $1000\text{V}$) apply karke cable insulation ki leakage test karta hai.
Q6. Motor winding ka ground test karne ke liye sabse best instrument kaun sa hai?
Ans: Megger Tester. Isse insulation puncture ka turant pata chal jata hai.
Q7. Multimeter ka selector switch kis line system ko set karne ke liye use hota hai?
Ans: Multiple functional ranges (Voltage, Current, Resistance) aur modes ke selection ke liye.
Q8. Kisi healthy winding ka minimum ideal insulation resistance kitna hona chahiye?
Ans: Kam se kam 1 Mega-Ohm ($1\text{ M}\Omega$) se upar hona chahiye.
Q9. Voltmeter ki measurement range ko extend karne ke liye kis element ka use karte hain?
Ans: High value Multiplier Resistor ko voltmeter coil ke series me joda jata hai.
Q10. Ammeter ki testing range badhane ke liye kya upaye kiya jata hai?
Ans: Ek low-resistance wire (Shunt) ko ammeter coil ke parallel me connected kiya jata hai.
