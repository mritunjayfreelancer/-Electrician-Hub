Module 11: Wiring Installation & Earthing (वायरिंग स्थापना और अर्थिंग)

Good Morning Students! Aaj ka class hamare poorae electrician trade ka sabse practical aur important topic hai—Wiring Installation guidelines aur Earthing Systems. Agar tumne is chapter ke safety concepts aur Indian Electricity (IE) Rules ko deep me samajh liya, to kisi bhi plant ya residential building me leakage aur fatal electric shocks ko 100% block kar doge. Ek-ek parameters ko dhyan se note karna!

Chapter 1: Wiring Installation & National Rules

Wiring Estimation Guidelines (स्थापना aur आकलन niyam)
Wiring installation must comply with National Electrical Code (NEC) and Indian Electricity Rules to ensure absolute safety against overloads and ground faults.
Hinglish Script: Kisi bhi ghar ya factory me wiring shuru karne se pehle hume load calculation aur cost estimation karna hota hai. Wires ka gauge (SWG - Standard Wire Gauge) hamesha load current ke mutabik select kiya jata hai. Copper conductors ka insulation aur current capacity aluminum se behtar hoti hai.
📜 Most Important IE Rules (भारतीय विद्युत नियम):
• Rule 50: Supply aur consumer ke beech insulation aur energy check locks perfect hone chahiye.
• Rule 77: Zameen se overhead lines ki minimum clear height standard bounds me honi chahiye.
• Rule 88: Guarding networks lagana mandatory hai taaki wire toote to seedhe zameen par na gire.

Chapter 2: What is Earthing & Why it matters?

Earthing Definition (अर्थिंग ki परिभाषा)
Earthing means connecting the metallic frame of electrical equipment to the earth plate/pipe embedded deep inside the ground using a low-resistance conductor.
Simple Classroom Concept: Jab kisi appliance (jaise Iron, Gyser, ya Motor) ki internal live insulation break ho jati hai, to current uski metallic body me aa jata hai. Agar koi insan use touch karega, to fatal shock lagega. Lekin agar metal body ko ek **Low Resistance wire** se seedhe zameen (Earth) se jod dein, to leakage current bina rukavat ke seedhe zameen me chala jata hai aur hum safe rehte hain!
💡 Objective Core Target: Earthing ka main objective hai equipment aur insani jaan ko protect karna. Safe human body ka average resistance lagbhag **1000 Ohms** hota hai, isiliye earth path ka resistance **bilkul minimum (near zero)** hona zaroori hai.

💡 Earth Resistance Kam Karne Ke Tarike

Agar soil dry ho jaye to earth resistance badh jata hai, isko control karne ke liye:
• Earth pit (गड्ढे) me regular interval par Paani (Watering) karte rehna chahiye.
• Wires/Electrodes ka contact surface badhane ke liye electroding depth badhaein.
• Chemical treatment ke liye zameen me Charcoal (लकड़ी ka कोयला) aur Salt (नमक) ki alternate layers dali jaati hain.
• Multiple earth electrodes ko aapas me Parallel Connection me joine se total internal resistance drop ho jata hai.

5. Master CBT Top 10 Exam Core Q&A Bank

Q1. Earth Resistance (पृथ्वी प्रतिरोध) ko measure karne ke liye kis instrument ka use karte hain?
Ans: Earth resistance ko test karne ke liye **Earth Tester (Fall of Potential Method)** ka use kiya jata hai jo ek micro-hand driven megger generator hota hai. Iska scale direct Ohm me hota hai.
Q2. Plate earthing me charcoals aur salt layers fill karne ka real objective kya hai?
Ans: Charcoal aur salt moisture (nami) ko absorb karke hold rakhte hain, jisse soil conductivity badh jaati hai aur earth resistance minimum bana rehta hai.
Q3. Heavy load handling main power generating stations me earth resistance ki maximum allowed limit kya hai?
Ans: Super large generation sites par absolute max limit **0.5 Ohm** se zyada nahi honi chahiye.
Q4. Pipe earthing installation system me perforation holes kyu diye jaate hain?
Ans: G.I. pipe par chote-chote holes isiliye kiye jaate hain taaki jab upar se funnel se paani dala jaye, to wo pipe se nikal kar charo taraf ki soil me barabar nami phaila sake.
Q5. Neutral Earthing aur Equipment Earthing me primary functional difference kya hai?
Ans: Equipment earthing metal body ko protect karti hai hume shock se bachane ke liye. Neutral earthing transformer ke neutral point ko zero voltage balance reference dene ke liye ki jaati hai.
Q6. Earthing wire ka size main line wire ke comparison me kaisa hona chahiye?
Ans: Earthing wire ka diameter main phase conductor wire ke sizing index se **hamesha bada ya barabar** hona chahiye taaki heavy fault current ko path safe mile. Minimum copper earthing wire range 14 SWG se kam nahi hona chahiye.
Q7. Indian Electricity Rule ke mutabik high voltage supply consumer entry terminals par consumer ke pass kya safety locks hone chahiye?
Ans: Consumer gate entries par mandatory protective **Main Switchgear Isolated Circuit Breakers** hone zaroori hain.
Q8. Soil resistivity (मिट्टी ka vishisht pratirodh) kis state me sabse high level par pahunch jati hai?
Ans: Extreme dry state yaani **Garmiyon ke mausam me ya dry sand area me** soil resistivity peaks hit karti hai, jise paani dalkar down kiya jata hai.
Q9. G.I. pipe elements aur accessories ko corrosion se bachane ke liye kis chemical coating process ka use kiya jata hai?
Ans: Hard metal configurations ko zinc hot layer bath process me rakha jata hai jise **Galvanization** kehte hain.
Q10. Do earthing electrodes ko parallel me connect karne se combination path ka output kaisa banta hai?
Ans: Parallel grouping se inverse reciprocal resistance law trigger hota hai, jisse overall net resistance **drastically minimize** ho jata hai.
