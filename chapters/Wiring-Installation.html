Wiring Installation & Earthing (वायरिंग इंस्टॉलेशन और अर्थिंग)

👨‍🏫 Classroom Note: Dosto, Wiring Installation aur Earthing competitive exams aur practical field dono ke liye sabse important core chapter hai. Is note me power station se electricity distribution ka pura process, wiring ke advanced styles, Earthing ke frameworks (Plate & Pipe) aur industrial IE Rules ko real classroom teacher style me compile kiya gaya hai!

Chapter 1 : Wiring Installation Kya Hoti Hai?

Power Station Se Ghar Tak Ka Safar

Imagine karo tumhare ghar me fan, bulb aur TV chal rahe hain. Ye electricity power station se hamare ghar tak ek sequential flow path me aati hai:

[ Power Station ] ↓ [ Transmission Line ] ↓ [ Distribution Transformer ] ↓ [ Service Line ] → [ Energy Meter ] → [ Main Switch ] ↓ [ Distribution Board ] → [ Individual Switches ] ↓ [ Load: Fan, Bulb, Socket ]
Wiring Installation Ki Definition
Electrical Wiring Installation is the complete arrangement of electrical wires, switches, sockets, protective devices and accessories used to safely distribute electrical power.
Aasan Bhasha me: Ghar ya building me electricity ko surakshit roop se distribute karne ke liye wire, switch, socket, fuse, aur MCB ke pure setup ko hi Wiring Installation kehte hain.

Objectives of a Good Wiring System

Ek adarsh (Good) wiring installation karte samay hume in 5 pramukh objectives ka dhyan rakhna chahiye:

🔒 1. Safety (सुरक्षा): Insaan ko electric shock aur building ko fire hazards se bachana.
⚡ 2. Reliability (विश्वसनीयता): Supply continuous milti rahe, koi internal circuit fault na ho.
💰 3. Economy (किफायती): Material quality achhi ho par kharcha budget ke andar ho.
🛠️ 4. Easy Maintenance: Fault hone par repair aasani se bina poori wiring kharab kiye kiya ja sake.
✨ 5. Good Appearance: Fitting dekhne me premium, neat aur sundar lage.
Industrial Design Objectives

Types of Wiring Systems (एग्जाम फेवरेट टॉपिक)

Syllabus me char pramukh purani aur aadhunik wiring ke types hain:

1. Cleat Wiring (क्लीट वायरिंग)

Vulkanized India Rubber (VIR) ya PVC wires ko Porcelain cleat ke support se deewar par open lagaya jata hai.
Pros/Cons: Cheap, Temporary aur fast installation. Lekin visually kharab, unprotected aur temporary use (Marriage, construction sites) ke liye hoti hai.

2. Batten Wiring (बैटन वायरिंग)

Lakdi ke flat wooden batten ko deewar par thoka jata hai, aur wire ko Link Clips ki help se fix kiya jata hai.
Pros/Cons: Neat lookup aur sasti hai, par wood moisture absorb karke short circuit aur fire risk badhata hai.

3. Casing and Capping Wiring

Wooden ya PVC ki V-shaped channel (Casing) ke andar wire dalkar upar se cover (Capping) lagayi jati hai. Modern PVC casing abhi bhi temporary offices me use hoti hai.

4. Conduit Wiring (कंड्यूट वायरिंग) - Most Important

Wires are drawn inside heavy duty PVC or Galvanized Iron (GI) pipes called conduits.

Surface Conduit: Pipe deewar ke upar dikhta hai (Industries).
Concealed Conduit: Pipe plaster ke andar chhupa hota hai (Modern Homes/Hospitals). Yeh fire-proof, safe aur long life hai.

IE Rules (Indian Electricity Rules) For Safety

The primary objective of Indian Electricity Rules is to safeguard people, animals, and properties from hazardous leakages and shocks.

Exam point of view se nimnlikhit niyam sabse jyada pucche jaate hain:

📌 Rule 1 (Insulation): Puri installation properly insulated honi chahiye. Kahin naked joint nahi hoga.
📌 Rule 2 (Earthing): Sabhi major metal body appliances (Cooler, Fridge) ko earth pin se jorna compulsory hai.
📌 Rule 3 (Main Switch): Emergency cut-off ke liye main switch building entrance ke bilkul paas hona chahiye.
📌 Rule 4 (Switch Location): Switch ko hamesha Phase (Live) wire me series me jodein, Neutral me kabhi nahi.
📌 Rule 5 (Fuse Integrity): Fuse hamesha Phase wire me lagega, Neutral me fuse wire lagana sakht mana hai.

Earthing Architecture (अर्थिंग सिस्टम)

Classroom Scenario Analysis:

Mano tumhare cooler ki internal copper binding toot kar metallic body ko touch kar gayi. Agar insulation fail hone par ground connection nahi hai to physical touch karte hi tez shock lagega. Lekin agar metal body earthed hai to fault current turant safety route se zameen me flow kar jayega, circuit ka fuse blow ho jayega ya MCB trip ho jayegi, aur aap safe rahenge.

Earthing is the process of connecting non-current carrying metallic parts of electrical equipment to the earth.

Plate Earthing vs Pipe Earthing (Deep Study)

Industrial framework me do methods sabse zyada chalte hain:

Method A: Plate Earthing

Isme 60cm × 60cm × 3.15mm ki Copper plate (ya 6mm ki GI plate) ko 3-metre gehre gaddhe (Pit) me vertical rakha jata hai. Plate ke charo taraf moisture maintain karne ke liye Charcoal (कोयला) aur Salt (नमक) ki alternate layers bichaayi jaati hain.

Method B: Pipe Earthing (Most Popular)

Isme perforated (holes wali) Galvanized Iron (GI) pipe (lagbhag 38mm diameter, 2.5m se 3m length) ko zameen me dump kiya jata hai. Yeh sasti hai aur maintenance asaan hai.

Plate Earthing vs Pipe Earthing Matrix

Parameter Plate Earthing Framework Pipe Earthing Framework
Core Element Copper or GI Solid Sheet Plate (600x600 mm) Perforated GI Hollow Pipe (38mm diameter)
Installation Cost Expensive (महंगा सिस्टम) Economical & Highly Cost-Effective
Pit Size Required Large dimension deep grid excavation Narrow drill pit setup
Resistance Value Extremely low resistance path Stable low resistance path (Most widely used)

Difference Between Earthing and Grounding

Feature Earthing (अर्थिंग) Grounding (ग्राउंडिंग)
Target Component Equipment ki non-current carrying metallic body ko jordata hai. System ke current carrying point (Jaise Transformer Neutral) ko jordata hai.
Primary Objective Human protection from dangerous leakage shock. System voltage stability aur fault current return line bypass control.
Standard Wire Color Green or Yellow-Green stripe line. Solid Black or Blue dependent network lines.

Industrial Oral Viva-Voce Questions Bank

Q1. Earthing pit me Salt aur Charcoal kyu dala jata hai?
Ans: Salt mitti ki electrical conductivity badhata hai aur Charcoal soil moisture (नमी) ko capture karke rakhta hai, jisse earth resistance permanently low bani rehti hai.
Q2. Ideal Earth Resistance ki standard value kitni honi chahiye?
Ans: Domestic structures ke liye 1 se 5 Ohm ke beech, aur badhe Power Stations/Substations me 1 Ohm se bhi kam (approx 0.5 Ohm) honi chahiye.

5. Master CBT Top 10 Exam Core Q&A Bank

Q1. Gharon me temporary decoration light setup ke liye kis type ki wiring upyukt hai?
Ans: Cleat Wiring (क्लीट वायरिंग). Yeh temporary aur asani se nikaali jaane wali wiring hai.
Q2. Modern concrete residential slabs me kis wiring ko primacy di jati hai?
Ans: Concealed Conduit Wiring. Yeh plaster ke andar hone ke karan fireproof aur neat look deti hai.
Q3. Phase, Neutral aur Earth wire ke naye standard color code kya hain?
Ans: Phase = Brown (भूरा), Neutral = Blue (नीला), Earth = Green/Yellow.
Q4. IE Rules ke mutabik kis line segment me security fuse lagana varjit (prohibited) hai?
Ans: Neutral wire me fuse lagana varjit hai, fuse hamesha keval Phase wire me lagna chahiye.
Q5. Earth electrode installation ke charo taraf mitti ko treat karne ka main logic kya hai?
Ans: Total internal earth resistance loop value ko minimum limit tak drop karna.
Q6. Plate Earthing setup me standard specification Copper plate ki ideal dimensions kya hoti hain?
Ans: 600 mm × 600 mm × 3.15 mm size parameter grid.
Q7. Electrical estimation process ka core beneficial utilization kya hota hai?
Ans: Execution se pehle material shortage aur unexpected financial financial losses se bachna.
Q8. Agar single switch load parameter leak hokar metal body par touch ho, to kya system protective device trip hoga?
Ans: Haan, agar safe low-resistance Earthing properly connected hai to MCB instantly trip ho jayegi.
Q9. System security frame me ELCB ka full name kya hota hai?
Ans: Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker. Yeh microscopic leakage current detection par bhi active trip karta hai.
Q10. Substations aur highly loaded generation grids ke liye ideal maximum grounding target resistance kitna hai?
Ans: Lagbhag 1 Ohm se kam, preferably down to 0.5 Ohm.
