Chapter 1: Domestic Wiring Methods & Systems

Types of Wiring Systems (वायरing प्रणाली ke प्रकार)
Domestic wiring involves installing conductors, switches, and protective devices safely. The most common methods include Cleat, Batten, and Conduit wiring systems.
Hinglish Framework: Kisi bhi building me electrical energy ko safe tarike se lights aur appliances tak pahunchane ke liye wiring ki jaati hai. Puraane zamane se lekar aaj ke advanced tech tak main 4 tarah ki systems hoti hain:

1. Cleat Wiring: Ye ek temporary wiring hoti hai jisme porcelain cleats ka use karke wires ko walls par khula dauda diya jata hai. Pise kam lagte hain par safety bohot kam hai.
2. Batten (CTS/TRS) Wiring: Isme lakdi (wood) ya PVC ki seedhi pattiyon (battens) par wires ko link clips se fix kiya jata hai. Purane gharo me ye bohot dikhti thi.
3. Conduit Wiring [Best & Modern]: Isme PVC ya GI (Galvanized Iron) ke pipes (conduits) ke andar se wires ko pass kiya jata hai. Ye do tarah ki hoti hai: *Surface* (deewar ke upar) aur *Concealed* (deewar ke andar chhipi hui). Mechanical damage aur aag se 100% protection milti hai.

Types of Switches & Applications

Switch Name (स्विच ka naam) Terminals Count Primary Installation Application (उपयोग)
One-Way Switch (एक-दिशीय स्विच) 2 Terminals Normal fan, light, ya socket ko ON/OFF karne ke liye.
Two-Way Switch (द्वि-दिशीय स्विच) 3 Terminals Staircase (jeene ki wiring) aur corridor me ek bulb ko do alag jagah se control karne ke liye.
Intermediate Switch 4 Terminals Ek bulb ya load ko 3 ya usse zyada alag-alag locations se control karne ke liye.
ICDP / ICTP Switches 2 / 3 Poles Main switchboards me total main phase aur neutral lines ko isolate karne ke liye.

Staircase Wiring Logic Framework

Staircase wiring allows controlling a single lamp from two distinct locations using two two-way switches.
Real Teacher Setup: Jeene ki wiring me hum do **Two-Way Switches** lagate hain. Jab tum neeche se upar jaoge, to neeche wala switch daba kar bulb ON kar sakte ho, aur upar pahunch kar upar wale switch se use OFF kar sakte ho. Isse electricity ki bachat hoti hai aur bar-bar neeche nahi aana padta.
🧠 CBT Exam Secret Key:
Two-way switch ke center terminal me hamesha phase line input ya bulb ka half-wire lagaya jata hai. Dono side ke terminals ko cross ya parallel wires se aapas me short kiya jata hai.

💡 Sahi Wiring Ke Niyam (IE Rules)

• Switch aur Fuse ki state: Hamesha neutral wire ko direct rakha jata hai aur saare switches aur fuses ko sub-circuit ke **Phase (Live) wire** par hi lagaya jata hai, taaki OFF hone par machine me current na bache.
• Sub-circuit Load Rules: Ek normal light/fan sub-circuit me max **10 points** ya **800 Watt** se zyada load nahi hona chahiye. Power sub-circuit me max **2 points** ya **3000 Watt** ka rule hota hai.

5. Master CBT Top 10 Exam Core Q&A Bank

Q1. Ghar ki wiring me link clips aur battens ka use kis type ki wiring me kiya jata hai?
Ans: Ye lakdi ya PVC patti par hone wali **Batten Wiring (CTS/TRS)** me use hota hai, jo sasti hoti hai par moisture me jaldi kharab ho jati hai.
Q2. Concealed Conduit Wiring ka sabse bada benefit kya hota hai?
Ans: Wires deewar ke plaster ke andar chhipe hote hain, isliye shock lagne ka khatra nahi hota, dekhne me sundar lagta hai, aur atmospheric safety 100% milti hai.
Q3. Staircase (jeene) ki wiring me kitne aur kaun se switches zaroori hain?
Ans: Isme **Do (2) Two-Way Switches** ki zaroorat hoti hai jisse ek single bulb ko do alag jagah se ON/OFF kiya ja sake.
Q4. Indian Electricity Rules ke mutabik light aur fan sub-circuit ka max load kitna hona chahiye?
Ans: Sub-circuit me max load **800 Watt** ya max **10 points (light/fan/socket combined)** se zyada nahi hona chahiye.
Q5. Earthing wire ka standard colour code kya hai aur ye kyu zaroori hai?
Ans: Earthing wire ka colour hamesha **Green (Hara)** hota hai. Ye leakage current ko turant zameen me bhej kar human body ko shock se bachata hai.
Q6. normal switch ko hamesha phase wire par hi kyu lagaya jata hai, neutral par kyu nahi?
Ans: Kyunki switch OFF hone par appliance tak aane wali live power cutoff ho jaye. Agar neutral par lagayenge to switch OFF hone par bhi machine me phase wire zinda rahega aur shock lag sakta hai.
Q7. Intermediate switch me total kitne terminals hote hain aur iska use kahan hota hai?
Ans: Intermediate switch me **Char (4) Terminals** hote hain. Iska use ek load ko 3 ya usse zyada alag-alag switchboard se control karne ke liye kiya jata hai.
Q8. ELCB / RCCB breaker hamare ghar me kya kaam karte hain?
Ans: Ye **Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker** hote hain. Agar ghar me kisi machine ki body me thoda sa bhi current leak hota hai ya kisi ko shock lagta hai, to ye 30 milli-seconds me poori bijli ko TRIP kar dete hain.
Q9. Godown (कबाड़खाना/गोदाम) wiring me kya special control loop mechanics hoti hai?
Ans: Godown wiring me linear combination switches use hote hain, jisme aage wala bulb ON karne par peeche wala bulb automatically OFF ho jata hai.
Q10. Temporary wiring ke liye kis method ko sabse best aur fast mana jata hai?
Ans: Temporary construction sites ya pandals me **Cleat Wiring** ki jaati hai kyunki ise install karna aur nikalna bohot fast aur sasta hota hai.
