Illumination (प्रदीप्ति / लाइटिंग)

👨‍🏫 Classroom Note: Hello Students! Aaj hum padhenge **Illumination (प्रदीप्ति)**। Simple words me kahein to kisi surface par jab light padti hai, to use illumination kehte hain। Exam me iske basic units, laws of illumination (Inverse Square Law), aur lamps ke types (LED, CFL, Sodium Vapor) par baar-baar questions aate hain। Ekदम dhyan se poora concept samajhna!

Chapter 1 : Basic Terms of Illumination

1. Light (प्रकाश)

Yeh ek prakar ki energy (radiant energy) hai jo hamari aankhon ko dekhne ki shakti pradan karti hai। Iski travel speed $3 \times 10^8\text{ m/s}$ hoti hai।

2. Luminous Flux (F or $\phi$)
Luminous Flux is the total quantity of light energy emitted per second by a light source.

Aasan Bhasha me: Koi bulb ya lamp pure 1 second me total jitni light nikalta hai, use Luminous Flux kehte hain। Iski Unit **Lumen** hoti hai (Exam Favourite)।

3. Luminous Intensity (I)

Kisi light source dwara kisi specific direction me per unit solid angle ($\omega$) me nikalne wala flux। Iski unit **Candela (Cd)** hoti hai। Formula: $I = \phi / \omega$.

Illumination (E) Aur Uske Units

Illumination (E) is defined as the luminous flux received per unit area of a surface.

Matalb: Jab kisi surface ke unit area par jitna flux padta hai, use Illumination kehte hain।

$$E = \frac{\text{Luminous Flux }(\phi)}{\text{Area }(A)} = \frac{\text{Lumen}}{\text{Meter}^2}$$
💡 Illumination ki standard Units:
• **Lumen/m²** (Ise hi hum **Lux** kehte hain)।
• **Lumen/ft²** (Ise **Foot-Candle** bola jata hai)।
• $1\text{ Lux} = 1\text{ Lumen/m}^2$.

Laws of Illumination (प्रदीप्ति के नियम)

Exam me in dono laws par numericals aur statements aate hain:

1. Inverse Square Law (व्युत्क्रम वर्ग का नियम)

Kisi surface par milne wala Illumination ($E$), source ki doori ($d$) ke square ke inversely proportional hota hai।

$$E \propto \frac{1}{d^2}$$

Matalb: Agar tum lamp se doori double (2 times) karoge, to light ka asar 4 guna kam ho jayega!

2. Lambert's Cosine Law (लैम्बर्ट का कोज्या नियम)

Illumination surface par padne wali light ke angle ke cosine ($\cos\theta$) ke directly proportional hota hai।

$$E = \frac{I \cdot \cos\theta}{d^2}$$

Types of Lamps (विभिन्न प्रकार के लैम्प)

Lamp Name Working Principle / Gas Used Luminous Efficiency Main Applications
Incandescent Lamp Tungsten Filament heat hokar light deta hai। (Inert gas: Nitrogen/Argon) 10–15 Lumen/Watt Domestic lighting (Old Yellow bulbs)
CFL (Compact Fluorescent) Mercury vapor excitement se UV rays banti hain, jo phosphor coating par light banati hain। 60–70 Lumen/Watt Homes, Offices (White Light)
LED (Light Emitting Diode) Semiconductor PN-Junction electronic light emission। (Most efficient) 100–120 Lumen/Watt Modern Homes, Street lights, Displays
Sodium Vapor Lamp Neon gas + Sodium particles। Yeh shuru me **Pink** aur full load par **Yellow (पीला)** light deta hai। 40–50 Lumen/Watt Street Lighting, Highways
Mercury Vapor Lamp High pressure mercury discharge। Yeh **Greenish-Blue** light deta hai। 30–40 Lumen/Watt Industrial yards, Parks, Railway sheds
Neon Sign Tube Neon Gas discharge। High voltage operate। Gives **Red/Orange** glow। 15–20 Lumen/Watt Advertising boards, Decoration

Important Terms For Exams

• Solid Angle ($\omega$): Yeh 3D angle hota hai, iski unit **Steradian** hoti hai।
• Depreciation Factor (मूल्यह्रास गुणांक): Dhool aur mitti jamne se light kam ho jati hai। Yeh hamesha 1 se bada (> 1) hota hai।
• Utilization Factor: Total output flux aur surface par pahunche flux ka ratio। Yeh hamesha 1 se kam (< 1) hota hai।
• Color Rendering Index (CRI): Light source kisi object ke real color ko kitna accurately dikha sakta hai (Scale: 0–100)।

Chapter Summary (One-Line Revision)

💡 Luminous Flux Unit = Lumen
🕯️ Luminous Intensity Unit = Candela (Cd)
📉 Illumination (E) Unit = Lux ($Lumen/m^2$)
📐 Inverse Square Law = $E \propto 1/d^2$
🟡 Sodium Vapor Lamp Light = Yellow (Full brightness par)
🔴 Neon Tube Light = Red/Orange (Advertising me useful)
⚡ Max Efficiency Lamp = LED Lamp (Light Emitting Diode)

Top 10 Exam Core Q&A Bank

Q1. Luminous Flux ki SI unit kya hoti hai?
Ans: Lumen। Yeh pure source dwara ek second me nikli light energy hai।
Q2. 1 Lux ka technical matlab kya hota hai?
Ans: $1\text{ Lux} = 1\text{ Lumen per square meter } (Lumen/m^2)$।
Q3. Sodium Vapor Lamp shuru karte hi kaun sa rang deta hai?
Ans: Shuruat me yeh halke Pink (गुलाबी) rang ka hota hai, aur fully garam hone par Yellow (पीला) rang deta hai।
Q4. Inverse Square Law ke anusar agar doori ko 3 guna badha diya jaye to illumination par kya farq padega?
Ans: Illumination 9 guna kam ho jayega ($E \propto 1/3^2 \implies 1/9$)।
Q5. Advertisement commercial boards me kis lamp ka use hota hai?
Ans: Neon Sign Tubes ka, kyunki isme attractive Red/Orange bright glow banta hai।
Q6. Filament lamps (Incandescent lamps) me kis material ka use filament ke liye hota hai?
Ans: Tungsten ka, kyunki iska melting point bohot high ($3422^\circ\text{C}$) hota hai।
Q7. Solid Angle ko naapne ki standard unit kya hai?
Ans: Steradian।
Q8. Sabse zyada luminous efficiency (Lumen/Watt) kis lamp ki hoti hai?
Ans: LED (Light Emitting Diode) lamp ki, yeh lagbhag 100–120 Lumen/Watt tak hoti hai।
Q9. G.I. pipes ya streets par lagne wale lamps me glare (चकाचौंध) kam karne ke liye kya lagate hain?
Ans: Reflectors aur Frosted globes ka use kiya jata hai taaki light barabar distribute ho।
Q10. Utilization Factor ka maan hamesha kitna hota hai?
Ans: Hamesha 1 se kam (< 1) hota hai, kyunki kuch light walls aur waste spaces me absorb ho jati hai।
