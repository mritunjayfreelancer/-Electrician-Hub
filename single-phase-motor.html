📖 Foundational Core

1. Core Definition

Simple Theory Mode (Hinglish)
Single Phase Motor ek electrical machine hai jo hamare gharon me aane wali standard 220V AC single phase supply par chalti hai. Yeh input electrical energy lekar use mechanical rotational energy me convert karti hai taaki load ghoom sake.
Exam Spec Specification:
"An electric motor is an electrical machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy."
🧠 Concept Engineering

2. The Self-Start Mystery (बड़ा इंटरव्यू सवाल)

Bhai, ek baat dimag me bitha lo: Single phase induction motors self-start nahi hotin! Agar tum pankhe ko bina capacitor ke direct supply doge, toh wo sirf humming sound (गूंजने की आवाज) karega par ghoom nahi payega.

Aisa kyu hota hai?

Chunki single phase supply stator windings me Rotating Magnetic Field (RMF) paida nahi karti, balki ek **Alternating/Pulsating Magnetic Field** paida karti hai. Is pulsating field ke karan rotor par net starting torque hamesha **Zero (0)** ho jata hai. Ise Double Revolving Field Theory kehte hain, jise niche dynamic engine me dekho:

🔬 FIELD ANALYSIS MATRIX: DOUBLE REVOLVING THEORY VECTOR HARMONICS
ROTOR
🔵 FORWARD COMPONENT (+T): 0 N
🔴 BACKWARD COMPONENT (-T): 0 N
⚡ NET STARTING TORQUE: 0 N (STATIC)

Circuit State: Disconnected

3. Single Phase Motor Types Classification

Pulsating field ko rotating banane ke liye hum stator slots me do windings lagate hain: **Main Winding** aur **Auxiliary (Starting) Winding**. Inke beech phase split karne ke alag-alag tariko ke base par yeh divide hoti hain:

4. Deep Internal Component Breakdowns

1. Split Phase Motor Logic: Isme starting winding ka resistance high aur reactance low rakha jata hai taaki phase difference ban sake.
Starting Torque: Medium
Uses: Fan, Grinder, Small Pump
2. Capacitor Start Motor Logic: Starting winding ke series me ek high-capacity electrolytic capacitor lagaya jata hai, jo motor ke $75\%$ speed pakadte hi Centrifugal Switch se cut ho jata hai.
Starting Torque: High
Uses: Air Compressor, Water Pump
3. Capacitor Run Motor Logic: Isme ek permanent capacitor hamesha auxiliary winding ke sath connected rehta hai. Isme koi centrifugal switch nahi hota.
Performance: Best Running Performance
Uses: Ceiling Fan, Exhaust Fan
4. Capacitor Start Capacitor Run Logic: Isme do capacitors use hote hain. Ek high value ka start karne ke liye (jo baad me hat jata hai) aur ek low value ka continuous running ke liye.
Starting Torque: Very High
Uses: Heavy Machines, Industrial loads
5. Shaded Pole Motor Logic: Iske poles par ek copper shading ring lagi hoti hai jo field ko shift karti hai. Yeh sabsse simple aur sasti motor hai.
Torque: Very Low Torque
Uses: Small Fan, Hair Dryer, Toys
6. Universal Motor Logic: Yeh motor AC aur DC dono supply lines par kam kar sakti hai. Iska internal structure DC Series motor ki tarah hota hai aur yeh bohot hi high speed par chalti hai.
Speed: Extremely High Speed
Uses: Mixer Grinder, Vacuum Cleaner, Drill Machine

5. Top 10 Viva & Interview Q&A Zone

Q1. Single Phase Motor kya hoti hai?
Ans: Electrical Energy ko Mechanical Energy (Rotation) me convert karne wali machine jo 220V AC lines par chale.
Q2. Kya single phase induction motor self-start hoti hai?
Ans: Bilkul nahi! Pulsating magnetic field ke karan iska initial starting torque zero hota hai.
Q3. Single phase motor me capacitor kyu lagate hain?
Ans: Phase ko split karne ke liye (taaki Main aur Auxiliary winding ke beech $90^\circ$ ka phase shift paida ho sake aur Rotating Magnetic Field ban jaye).
Q4. Ghar ke ceiling fan me kaunsi motor hoti hai?
Ans: Permanent Capacitor Run Single Phase Induction Motor.
Q5. Centrifugal switch ka kya kaam hota hai?
Ans: Motor ke $75\% - 80\%$ speed gain karne par starting capacitor aur starting winding ko circuit se safely disconnect karna.
Q6. Universal Motor kis supply par chalti hai?
Ans: Yeh AC aur DC dono supply systems par perfectly run karti hai.
Q7. Mixer Grinder me kaunsi motor hoti hai?
Ans: Universal Motor (kyunki isme high speed aur heavy load driving capacity chahiye).
Q8. Sabse sasti aur kam maintenance wali motor kaunsi hai?
Ans: Shaded Pole Motor.
Q9. Agar run karte time ceiling fan ka capacitor hata dein toh kya hoga?
Ans: Fan chalta rahega par uski speed bohot kam ho jayegi aur winding garam hone lagegi.
Q10. Single phase motor ki direction kaise reverse kar sakte hain?
Ans: Ya toh Main Winding ya fir Auxiliary Winding me se kisi BHI EK ke connections ko aapas me reverse karke.
