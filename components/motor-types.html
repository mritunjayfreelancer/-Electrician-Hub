📖 Foundational Core

1. Core Machine Definition

Motor ek electrical machine hai jo Electrical Energy ko Mechanical Energy (Rotation) me convert karti hai.

Simple Definition (Exam Spec):
"An electric motor is an electrical machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy."

2. Motor Kitne Type Ki Hoti Hai?

Motor ko supply ke hisab se 2 main types me divide kiya jata hai:

ELECTRIC MOTOR │ ┌───────────────┴───────────────┐ │ │ DC MOTOR AC MOTOR
🔋 Direct Current Domain

3. 1️⃣ DC Motor (Direct Current Motor)

Definition: Jo motor DC Supply (Battery, DC Power Supply) se chalti hai use DC Motor kehte hain.

Kaha Use Hoti Hai? Electric Vehicle, Crane, Lift, Toy Car, Conveyor, Railway.

Types of DC Motor Branch Matrix

DC MOTOR │ ├── Separately Excited Motor ├── Shunt Motor ├── Series Motor ├── Compound Motor └── PMDC Motor
1. Separately Excited DC Motor

Definition: Jis motor me Field Winding aur Armature ko alag-alag supply di jati hai use Separately Excited Motor kehte hain.

✔ Speed Control Best | ✔ Industrial Use | ✔ Accurate Control
Applications: Steel Plant, Paper Mill, Laboratory
2. DC Shunt Motor

Definition: Jis motor ki Field Winding Armature ke Parallel (Shunt) connected hoti hai use Shunt Motor kehte hain.

✔ Constant Speed | ✔ Smooth Running | ✔ Medium Starting Torque
Applications: Fan, Lathe Machine, Blower, Pump
3. DC Series Motor

Definition: Jis motor ki Field Winding Armature ke Series me connected hoti hai use Series Motor kehte hain.

✔ Very High Starting Torque | ✔ Heavy Load Start Kar Sakti Hai
⚠ Safety Warning: No Load Par Kabhi Mat Chalao (Speed dangerous ho sakti hai)
Applications: Crane, Lift, Electric Train, Hoist
4. DC Compound Motor

Definition: Jis motor me Series aur Shunt dono Field Winding hoti hain use Compound Motor kehte hain.

Sub-types: Cumulative Compound aur Differential Compound
Applications: Elevator, Press Machine, Rolling Mill
5. PMDC Motor (Permanent Magnet DC Motor)

Definition: Jis motor me Field Winding ki jagah Permanent Magnet use hota hai.

✔ Small Size | ✔ Light Weight | ✔ High Efficiency
Applications: Toy, Car Wiper, Power Window, Battery Operated Devices
🏠 Alternating Current (Home Range)

4. 2️⃣ AC Motor - Single Phase Network

Definition: Jo motor AC Supply se chalti hai use AC Motor kehte hain.

AC MOTOR │ ├── Single Phase Motor (220V - Home Use) └── Three Phase Motor (440V - Industrial Use)

A. Single Phase Motor Sub-categories

Single Phase Motor │ ├── Split Phase ├── Capacitor Start ├── Capacitor Run ├── Capacitor Start Capacitor Run ├── Shaded Pole └── Universal Motor
Split Phase Motor Torque: Medium
Uses: Fan, Grinder, Small Pump
Capacitor Start Motor Torque: High
Uses: Air Compressor, Water Pump
Capacitor Run Motor Performance: Best Running Style
Uses: Ceiling Fan, Exhaust Fan
Capacitor Start Capacitor Run Torque: Very High Torque
Uses: Heavy Machines
Shaded Pole Motor Features: Cheap, Simple, Low Torque
Uses: Small Fan, Hair Dryer, Toy
Universal Motor Features: Runs on AC and DC Both, High Speed
Uses: Mixer Grinder, Vacuum Cleaner, Drill Machine
🏭 Industrial Automation Grids

5. B. Three Phase Motor Classification

Yeh motors 440 Volt Industrial Supply var work karti hain aur high mechanical efficiency deliver karti hain.

Three Phase Motor ├── Squirrel Cage Induction Motor ├── Slip Ring Motor ├── Synchronous Motor ├── Servo Motor ├── Stepper Motor ├── BLDC Motor └── PMSM
Squirrel Cage Motor
✔ Most Popular | ✔ Low Cost | ✔ Low Maintenance | ✔ Strong Construction
Applications: Factory, Pump, Compressor, Conveyor
Slip Ring Motor
✔ High Starting Torque | ✔ Rotor Resistance Control capabilities
Applications: Crane, Lift, Crusher, Rolling Mill
Synchronous Motor
✔ Constant Speed | ✔ Slip = Zero constant operation
Applications: Power Factor Improvement, Large Industries
Servo Motor
✔ Position Control | ✔ High Accuracy parameters
Applications: Robot, CNC Machine, Automation
Stepper Motor
✔ Step by Step Rotation execution lines
Applications: 3D Printer, CNC, Camera
BLDC Motor
✔ No Brush architecture | ✔ High Efficiency | ✔ Low Maintenance
Applications: Drone, Electric Bike, Computer Fan
PMSM (Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor)
✔ Very High Efficiency matrix | ✔ Optimized for EV
Applications: Electric Car, Electric Scooter, Industrial Drives

6. 📝 Core Interview Q&A Zone

Q1. Motor kya hoti hai?
Ans: Electrical Energy ko Mechanical Energy me convert karne wali machine.
Q2. Motor kitne type ki hoti hai?
Ans: Main do categories hain: DC Motor aur AC Motor.
Q3. AC Motor kitne type ki hoti hai?
Ans: Single Phase aur Three Phase.
Q4. Sabse zyada industry me kaunsi motor use hoti hai?
Ans: Squirrel Cage Induction Motor.
Q5. Sabse high starting torque kis motor ka hota hai?
Ans: DC Series Motor.
Q6. Constant speed kis motor ki hoti hai?
Ans: DC Shunt Motor aur Synchronous Motor.
Q7. Universal Motor kis supply par chalti hai?
Ans: AC aur DC dono supply lines par chalti hai.
Q8. EV me kaunsi motor use hoti hai?
Ans: BLDC aur PMSM.
Q9. Home me fan me kaunsi motor hoti hai?
Ans: Capacitor Run Single Phase Induction Motor.
Q10. Crane me kaunsi motor use hoti hai?
Ans: DC Series Motor ya Slip Ring Induction Motor.
